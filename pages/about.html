About SemesterMate

SemesterMate is a student productivity platform designed to help engineering students manage attendance, CGPA, study sessions and academic resources in one place.

Our Mission

To provide free academic tools and study resources for students.

Features

  • Attendance Predictor
  • CGPA Calculator
  • Target CGPA Calculator
  • Pomodoro Timer
  • Countdown Manager
  • Notes Portal
  • Student Blog
